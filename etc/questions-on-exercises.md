# Exercises

- How to keep track of bellows? Hanon exercises instruct to change bellows
  every 4th measure. What is the significance of this? The exercises
  don't have phrases to keep track of bellows with.
  Answer: The bellows should move with a phrase. In a hannon exercise this
  is arbitrary.

- Looking for various arpeggio exercises for improvising on chords.
  Any recomendations?
  Answer: Blues scale

- I struggle with the mechanics of getting into position for alternating
  bass such as in Hanon exercise 2.
  Answer: This is naturally getting easier.

- My hand feels more free to move when opening bellows. When closing bellows
  my wrist is attached to the accordion and I can't move my arm, only my
  wrist.
  Answer: The forearm should have some movement on opening and closing bellows.
  In order to do this the left hand strap needs to be tighter than I had it.
